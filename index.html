Little Nightmares II is a suspense adventure game in which you play as Mono, a young boy trapped in a
world that has been distorted by an evil transmission. Together with his new friend Six, he sets out to discover
the source of the transmission.

The Hunter is found in the forest. Mono has to avoid the traps that have been left to capture anyone who finds themselves in them whilst he is being hunted.

The Teacher is found in the school where she teaches the bullies who torment Mono and Six. They have to escape as the teacher tries to discipline them.

The Doctor is found in the hospital where he works on his experiments. Mono has to navigate the halls avoiding the patients within.

The Thin Man is found in the city. There is not much known about him other than he is using some kind of power to try to control Mono and Six.
